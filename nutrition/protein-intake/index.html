Protein Intake Calculator

Enter your weight and training goal to calculate your ideal daily protein intake.

How it works

Protein multipliers are applied based on activity: 1.6–2.2 g/kg for muscle gain, 1.8–2.4 g/kg for fat loss, and 2.0–2.8 for athletes.

How to interpret

Hitting the upper range supports recovery and hypertrophy. Use your total caloric target to adjust final intake.
