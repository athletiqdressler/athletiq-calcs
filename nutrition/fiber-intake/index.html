Fiber Intake Calculator

Estimate your ideal daily fiber intake based on calorie intake or body weight.

How it works

Two models are used: 14g of fiber per 1000 kcal, or 0.25–0.35g/kg of body weight.

How to interpret

Hitting the lower end improves digestion; higher intake improves satiety and glucose control.
