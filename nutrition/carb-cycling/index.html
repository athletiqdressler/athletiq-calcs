Carb Cycling Planner

Generate low, medium, and high-carb days based on TDEE and training intensity.

How it works

Carb cycling assigns different carb percentages: low (20–30%), medium (35–45%), high (50–60%).

How to interpret

Higher carb days align with intense training. Lower carb days improve metabolic flexibility.
