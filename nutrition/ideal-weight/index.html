Ideal Weight Calculator

Use evidence-based formulas (Devine & Hamwi) to estimate your ideal weight for performance, aesthetics, and health.

How it works

The calculator applies the Devine and Hamwi equations to estimate ideal body weight ranges based on height and biological sex.

How to interpret

Use the value as a reference point, not a mandatory target. Athletes may sit above or below ideal weight depending on muscle mass.
