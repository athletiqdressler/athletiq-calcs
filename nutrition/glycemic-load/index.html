Glycemic Load Calculator

Determine the real glycemic impact of a serving using GI and carbohydrate content.

How it works

GL = (GI × grams of carbs) ÷ 100. Range defines low, medium, or high glycemic load.

How to interpret

Low: <10, Medium: 10–19, High: ≥20. Athletes may use high GL pre-training.
