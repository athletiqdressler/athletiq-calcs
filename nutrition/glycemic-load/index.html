Glycemic Load Calculator

Enter GI and grams of carbs to calculate glycemic load instantly.

How it works

GL = (GI × grams of carbohydrates) ÷ 100. It measures the actual blood-sugar impact of a serving.

How to interpret

Low GL < 10, Medium 10–19, High ≥ 20. Athletes may use higher GL for fueling prior to training.
