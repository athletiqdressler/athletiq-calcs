How it works

After calculating protein and fat calories, leftover calories become carbohydrates. Days are broken down into low (75%), medium (100%) and high (125%) carb days.

How to interpret

Useful for energy management: more carbs on intense days, fewer on recovery days for improved metabolic flexibility.
