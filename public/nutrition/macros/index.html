How it works

The calculator uses your body weight to set protein and allocates a portion of your calories to fat. The remaining calories are assigned to carbohydrates. Ratios are adjusted according to your goal.

How to interpret the results

Use the macro targets as a starting point. Track performance, recovery and adherence, then adjust slightly if needed. Consistency matters more than hitting exact numbers every single day.
