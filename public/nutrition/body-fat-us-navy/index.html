How it works

The US Navy method uses logarithmic equations that combine height and circumference measurements to estimate body fat percentage. It is a practical field method, not a lab-grade measurement.

How to interpret the results

Compare your result with typical ranges for your sex and age. Track changes over time rather than obsessing over a single value. Training consistency and performance trends matter more than one estimate.
