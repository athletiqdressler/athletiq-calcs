How it works

Glycemic load (GL) combines the glycemic index (GI) of a food with the amount of carbohydrates consumed. Formula: GL = (GI × available carbs per serving) / 100.

How to interpret

GL < 10 = low impact. GL 10–19 = moderate. GL ≥ 20 = high impact. Useful for managing energy levels and appetite.
