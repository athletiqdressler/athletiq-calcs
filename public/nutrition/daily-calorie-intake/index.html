How it works

First, the calculator uses the Mifflin-St Jeor equation to estimate your BMR. Then it multiplies it by an activity factor to estimate your TDEE and adjusts the result according to your goal.

How to interpret the results

The suggested calories are an estimate, not a rigid rule. Track your weight and adjust intake gradually if you are not progressing as expected. Combine the plan with resistance training and adequate sleep.
