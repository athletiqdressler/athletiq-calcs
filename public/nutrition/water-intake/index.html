How it works

The calculator uses a base recommendation of around 35 ml of water per kilogram of body weight and adds a small multiplier depending on your activity level.

How to interpret the results

The result is a practical target, not a strict rule. Climate, sweat rate and diet also influence your needs. Use your thirst, urine color and performance as feedback signals.
