How it works

The Cooper test asks you to run as far as possible in 12 minutes. VO2max is estimated with the equation: VO2max = (distance in meters − 504.9) ÷ 44.73.

How to interpret

Higher VO2max generally means better aerobic fitness. Compare your value with reference tables for your age and sex, and track progress over time.
