How it works

The planner uses simple rules: small weekly jumps in load (2.5–5%), one extra rep per set, or one extra set at the same load depending on the chosen strategy.

How to interpret

Overload should be gradual and sustainable. If technique or recovery suffer, repeat the same workload or take a deload week instead of forcing progression.
