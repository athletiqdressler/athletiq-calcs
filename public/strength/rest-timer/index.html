How it works

The timer counts down from your chosen rest duration and signals when time is up. It runs in the browser and resets each time you start a new rest.

How to interpret

Consistent rest periods keep training density and performance predictable. Longer rests generally support heavier sets; shorter rests increase metabolic stress.
