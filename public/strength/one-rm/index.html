How it works

The calculator uses two common equations to estimate your 1RM: Epley and Brzycki. Both extrapolate your max from a submaximal set performed close to failure, typically between 2 and 12 reps.

How to interpret

Use 1RM estimates to plan training loads by percentage. Keep in mind that fatigue, technique and exercise choice affect the result, so use it as a guideline rather than an exact number.
