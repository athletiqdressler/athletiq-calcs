How it works

A simple rule of thumb: running burns about 1 kcal per kg of body weight per kilometer. Calories ≈ weight(kg) × distance(km).

How to interpret

Use this as a rough estimate for planning energy intake and comparing runs. Terrain, pace and efficiency change actual values.
