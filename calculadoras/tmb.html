BMR / TMB Calculator

Your basal metabolic rate estimates how many calories your body burns at complete rest. Use this as the baseline to build your deficit or surplus.

How to use this number

• BMR is the baseline. Real daily expenditure is higher (TDEE).
• For fat loss, people usually aim for 300–500 kcal below TDEE.
• For gaining muscle, 200–400 kcal above TDEE, with serious training.
