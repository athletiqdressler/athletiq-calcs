How it works

Weekly hard sets per muscle group are a key driver of hypertrophy. This tool sums your sets across up to four weekly sessions to give a clear picture of workload.

How to interpret

Many lifters progress well around 10–20 hard sets per muscle per week. Adjust based on your recovery, strength gains and joint health.
